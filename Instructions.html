Q:How do I add partners?

A: Head to the Add/Search Partners tab, and type information into the apropriate boxes.

Q:How do I search for a specific partner?

A: Type any part of the partner's name into the search bar, and click search.

Q: What does generate report do?

A: The Generate Report page allows you to download a report to your laptop of all the entered business partners.
